About Optimas Robotics Lab

Empowering university students with hands-on robotics education and real-world innovation.

Our Mission

To ignite curiosity and cultivate technical skills in robotics through accessible, practical, and inclusive education.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s leading center for robotics innovation, research, and training.

What We Do

Optimas Robotics Lab is a premier space for students to explore and build the future of robotics. From Arduino and Raspberry Pi projects to AI and computer vision, our programs blend theory with hands-on labs. Whether you’re a beginner or advanced learner, we equip you with the skills, mentorship, and real-world experience needed to innovate.
