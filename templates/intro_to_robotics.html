  • Definition and History of Robotics
    Robotics deals with the design, construction, and operation of robots. From ancient myths to modern industrial bots like Unimate, robotics has transformed industries and daily life.
  • Types of Robots
    • Industrial Robots: For repetitive manufacturing tasks like welding and assembly.
    • Service Robots: Personal and professional helpers like Roomba or reception bots.
    • Medical Robots: Assisting surgery, diagnosis, and rehabilitation.
    • Autonomous Vehicles: Self-driving cars and drones using sensors and AI.
  • Applications in Real-World Scenarios
    • Manufacturing: Robotic arms improve speed and safety.
    • Healthcare: Assistive and surgical robots.
    • Agriculture: Precision farming with drones and robots.
  • Ethical and Societal Impacts
    Concerns include job displacement, safety, privacy, and ethical AI. Inclusive and responsible design is crucial.
